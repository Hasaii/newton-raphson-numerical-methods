Newton-Raphson Numerical Method

Mathematical Discussion

The Newton-Raphson Method is a numerical method used to approximate the roots of nonlinear equations of the form:

\[ f(x)=0 \]

The method begins with an initial approximation \(x_0\) and repeatedly improves the estimate using tangent lines to the function.

The iterative formula for the Newton-Raphson Method is:

\[ x_{n+1} = x_n - \frac{f(x_n)}{f'(x_n)} \]

where:

  • \(f(x)\) is the original function
  • \(f'(x)\) is the derivative of the function
  • \(x_n\) is the current approximation
  • \(x_{n+1}\) is the improved approximation

The process continues until the difference between successive approximations becomes sufficiently small.

Advantages

  • Fast convergence near the root
  • High accuracy
  • Efficient for many engineering problems

Disadvantages

  • Requires computation of derivatives
  • May fail if the initial guess is poor
  • Fails when \(f'(x)=0\)

Worked Example 1

Solve the equation:

\[ f(x)=x^2-2 \]

using the Newton-Raphson Method with initial guess:

\[ x_0=1 \]

Step 1: Derivative

\[ f'(x)=2x \]

Step 2: Newton-Raphson Formula

\[ x_{n+1}=x_n-\frac{f(x_n)}{f'(x_n)} \]

Iteration 0

\[ x_1 = 1-\frac{1^2-2}{2(1)} \]

\[ x_1=1-\frac{-1}{2} \]

\[ x_1=1.5 \]

Iteration 1

\[ x_2=1.5-\frac{(1.5)^2-2}{2(1.5)} \]

\[ x_2=1.5-\frac{0.25}{3} \]

\[ x_2=1.416667 \]

Iteration 2

\[ x_3=1.416667-\frac{(1.416667)^2-2}{2(1.416667)} \]

\[ x_3\approx1.414216 \]

Final Answer: \[ \sqrt{2}\approx1.414216 \]

Worked Example 2

Solve the equation:

\[ f(x)=x^3-x-2 \]

using Newton-Raphson Method with:

\[ x_0=1.5 \]

Step 1: Derivative

\[ f'(x)=3x^2-1 \]

Step 2: Newton-Raphson Formula

\[ x_{n+1}=x_n-\frac{x_n^3-x_n-2}{3x_n^2-1} \]

Iteration 0

\[ x_1=1.5-\frac{1.5^3-1.5-2}{3(1.5)^2-1} \]

\[ x_1=1.5-\frac{-0.125}{5.75} \]

\[ x_1=1.521739 \]

Iteration 1

\[ x_2=1.521739-\frac{(1.521739)^3-1.521739-2}{3(1.521739)^2-1} \]

\[ x_2\approx1.521380 \]

Iteration 2

\[ x_3\approx1.521380 \]

The values no longer change significantly.

Final Answer: \[ x\approx1.521380 \]
